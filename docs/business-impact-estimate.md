# Business Impact Estimate

## One-Slide Investor Version

### Health AI: Back-of-the-Envelope Impact for 1 District Pilot

**Pilot volume assumption**
- 10,000 interactions per month
- 6,000 symptom checks
- 2,500 prescription explanations
- 1,500 hospital-routing requests

**Core assumptions**
- 20% of symptom checks avoid one unnecessary hospital or clinic trip
- Each avoided trip saves 3 hours and Rs120 travel cost
- Patient time value = Rs40 per hour
- Staff time saved:
  - 4 minutes per prescription explanation
  - 4 minutes per pre-triaged consult on 3,000 cases
  - 3 minutes per routing request
- Staff time value = Rs250 per hour
- 15% of symptom checks are true consult-needed cases
- 20% of those are recovered into formal consultations
- Net contribution per recovered consult = Rs350
- 35% of recovered consults generate an additional Rs250 diagnostics or pharmacy contribution

**Estimated monthly impact**
- 1,200 unnecessary trips avoided
- 4,042 total hours saved
- Rs3.98 lakh cost reduction
- Rs0.79 lakh provider revenue recovered
- Rs4.77 lakh total monthly value created

**Estimated annual impact**
- About 48,500 hours saved
- Rs47.8 lakh cost reduction
- Rs9.5 lakh revenue recovered
- Rs57.3 lakh total annual value

**Why this matters**
- Reduces avoidable outpatient load
- Improves routing of real cases into formal care
- Saves rural families travel time and direct cost
- Creates measurable ROI even before full clinical integration

## Calculation Logic

### Time Saved
- Avoided patient trips = 6,000 x 20% = 1,200 per month
- Patient time saved = 1,200 x 3 hours = 3,600 hours per month
- Prescription support time saved = 2,500 x 4 min = 167 hours per month
- Pre-triage clinician time saved = 3,000 x 4 min = 200 hours per month
- Routing support time saved = 1,500 x 3 min = 75 hours per month
- Total time saved = 3,600 + 167 + 200 + 75 = 4,042 hours per month

### Cost Reduced
- Travel cost avoided = 1,200 x Rs120 = Rs1.44 lakh per month
- Patient time-value saved = 3,600 x Rs40 = Rs1.44 lakh per month
- Staff productivity value = 442 x Rs250 = about Rs1.10 lakh per month
- Total cost reduction = Rs3.98 lakh per month

### Revenue Recovered
- Consult-needed cases = 6,000 x 15% = 900 per month
- Recovered formal visits = 900 x 20% = 180 per month
- Consult contribution = 180 x Rs350 = Rs0.63 lakh per month
- Diagnostics or pharmacy contribution = 180 x 35% x Rs250 = about Rs0.16 lakh per month
- Total revenue recovered = Rs0.79 lakh per month

## Scaling View

### If expanded to 5 districts with similar usage
- Cost reduction = about Rs1.99 crore per year
- Revenue recovered = about Rs47.5 lakh per year
- Total value = about Rs2.87 crore per year

## Notes
- This is a directional estimate, not an audited forecast.
- The largest value driver is usually avoided travel and lower frontline workload, not direct monetization.
- If the deployment is government-led, the primary KPI is likely cost-to-serve reduction and improved access.
- If the deployment is provider-led, the primary KPI is recovered consultations and better downstream conversion.
